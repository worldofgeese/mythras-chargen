3

Folk Magic is common, small, and practical

AiG p.63-68; REF folk-magic-aig.json

Folk Magic is the first magic most characters learn. It is usually cheap, brief, and useful before or during everyday danger.

  • Pick three cultural folk spells in chargen and two more if your career grants them.
  • Write down the spell effect and your table's casting roll before play.
  • Common tactical spells include Bladesharp, Protection, Heal, Mobility, and Fanaticism.
4

Theism: miracles come from your cult

AiG p.69-122; NfP one-pagers; ADR-002

As an initiate, record the miracles your character learned or selected in chargen. Write each miracle's category, rune, cost, and recovery rule; your rune choices determine which miracles you can reliably cast.

  • Common miracles and cult miracles are separate categories in the reference data.
  • Associated-cult miracles are one-time use until you return to the associated cult and resacrifice; mark them clearly on your sheet.
  • Long-duration miracles can tie up your Devotional Pool until they end.
Attestable chain: Notes from Pavis PDF -> references/theism-miracles.json -> inline MIRACLES_DATA -> Step 9 miracle picker.
5

Animism: spirits are characters, not spell names

MYT p.131-154; MI/BiH spirit refs; ADR-0006

Animists bind spirits into fetishes and command them. At this table, "binding a spirit" uses the Spirit Rune replacement for the printed Binding skill; Trance is still relevant for perceiving, negotiating with, and entering spirit-world situations. Know what each spirit can do, its intensity, and whether it is useful in combat, healing, scouting, or protection.

  • Spirit slots at chargen use CHA/2 for Spirit Worshipper rank.
  • Spirit examples come from Monster Island and A Bird in the Hand reference JSON.
  • Bring the spirit's ability text to the table; do not expect the GM to look it up for you.
6

Sorcery: flexible, deliberate, and expensive

MYT p.162-178; AiG p.60; REF sorcery.json

Sorcery separates the spell from its shaping. At this table, the spell's Rune Affinity handles Invocation and the Law Rune handles Shaping.

  • Starting sorcerers choose three spells in chargen.
  • Before a session, write one fast version and one full version of each spell.
  • In combat, over-shaping can cost you the action economy.
7

Mysticism status

MYT mysticism; ADR-0006; ADR-0007

Mysticism support exists so the app can grow with future cult data. No currently published supported cult in this campaign route requires it at character creation.

If a future handout or one-pager introduces mysticism, it must be added through the data-attestation chain before it becomes player-facing.
