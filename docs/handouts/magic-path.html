1

Learn the four sheet terms first

Adventures in Glorantha p.59-60; Mythras Core p.131-154 and p.162-178

Rune Affinity is your percentage for a Gloranthan rune, such as Air, Earth, Death, Spirit, or Law. Adventures in Glorantha introduces rune-based magic and affinities; our table uses them to make Gloranthan magic feel different from default Mythras skill names.

Spirit Rune is the Rune Affinity used when an animist binds or commands a spirit at this table. Trance still matters for sensing, entering, and navigating spirit-world situations.

Invocation is the Mythras Core sorcery skill for casting a sorcery spell. Sorcery spells in this campaign do not have assigned rune affinities, even when they come from a source-backed path such as Zzistori.

Shaping is the Mythras Core sorcery skill for changing a spell's range, duration, targets, magnitude, or other parameters.

Mysticism is the Mythras Core discipline for mystic talents. It is not cult Rune Magic, spirit binding, or sorcery; read it as its own tradition.

3

Folk Magic is the shared starter layer

Adventures in Glorantha p.63-68

Most characters know a few practical spells. Before play, write each folk spell's effect, cost, duration, and whether you expect to cast it before or during danger.

  • Good first reads: Bladesharp, Protection, Heal, Mobility, Fanaticism, and Coordination.
  • Do not try to memorize the whole list. Know your own spells first.
4

Theism: miracles come from cult commitment

Adventures in Glorantha p.69-122; cult one-pagers

Theist characters learn miracles through cults. Bring the miracle name, rune, category, cost, and recovery rule to the table.

  • Any Rune or normal cult miracles: learned through your cult and recovered through worship, sacrifice, and access to an appropriate shrine, temple, or holy place.
  • Subservient cult miracles: tied to a related subcult or servant cult; expect temple access and cult obligation to matter.
  • Associated cult miracles: one-use until you return to the associated cult and make the needed offering again.
5

Animism: spirits are helpers with abilities

Mythras Core p.131-154; Monster-Island.pdf p.129-136; A Bird in the Hand p.43-46

Animists bind spirits into fetishes and command them for help. A spirit is not just a spell name: it has intensity, characteristics, abilities, and limits. A Shaman career gives you independent career Animism; Waha gives cult-backed Animism alongside cult Rune Magic.

  • Write each spirit's ability in plain language: heal, bless, scout, guard, fight spirits, or reveal hidden things.
  • Track your bound spirit slots. A starting Spirit Worshipper uses CHA/2 slots.
  • Read examples in A-Bird-in-the-Hand.pdf p.43-47 and spirit cult structure in Monster-Island.pdf PDF p.129-140.
Monster Island table rules: Monster Island is a harsher, slower magic environment. PDF p.129-132 says magical energy recovers slowly and all living creatures recover one Magic Point per day on the island. PDF p.130 adds Casting Time Difficulty for rushed Sorcery and Theism; it explicitly says Animism has no change to Trance duration, though totemic binding rituals can use orchestration. PDF p.138 changes Nature Spirit Dominate Species to control combined SIZ equal to ten times Intensity. PDF p.168 lists Kahuna's Bane as incense that blocks access to the Spirit World. The character generator records your starting spirits and magic choices; it does not enforce Monster Island recovery rates, ritual timing, pacts, or poisons.
6

Sorcery: prepare simple versions before play

Mythras Core p.162-178

Sorcery is flexible, but that flexibility can slow the table. A Sorcerer career and a source-backed path such as Zzistori both use the same core skills: Invocation to cast and Shaping to modify. For each known spell, write one fast version and one shaped version, and note the Invocation and Shaping percentages on your sheet.

  • Fast version: the minimum casting you can use under pressure.
  • Shaped version: the same spell with planned changes to range, duration, targets, or magnitude, using your Shaping skill.
  • Monster Island: if the campaign is using the island's magic ecology, Sorcery and Theism may need ceremonial casting times or suffer the Casting Time Difficulty table. Ask the GM before assuming you can rush a shaped spell.
6b

Mysticism: use the RAW discipline

Mythras Core p.154-161

Mysticism is not a cult spell list. If your sheet says Mystic, read only the talents you selected, your Mysticism skill, the Magic Point cost if any, and whether a benefit is passive or must be activated.
