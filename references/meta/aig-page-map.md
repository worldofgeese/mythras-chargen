### AiG-Specific Additions to Base Mythras
- **Rune Affinities** (Step 5): 3 runes, POW×2+30/20/10% — p.24
- **Folk Magic** (Step 5): POW+CHA+30%, 3 starting spells from culture list — p.24
- **Additional Folk Magic** (Step 9): 2 more spells from Folk Magic chapter — p.24
- **Tradetalk**: All characters get Tradetalk at INT+CHA+30% — p.24
- **Starting Money**: Culture-specific multiplier table — p.25
- **Combat Styles**: Glorantha-specific, tied to culture + career — p.26
